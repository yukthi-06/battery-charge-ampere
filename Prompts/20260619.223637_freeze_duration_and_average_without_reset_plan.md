# Implementation Plan - Freeze Duration and Average without Reset

## Problem
In the previous implementation, the broadcast receiver was resetting the measurement session (setting duration and average to zero) on both charger connection and disconnection. When the charger is disconnected, the duration and average should freeze at their current values instead of resetting to zero.

## Proposed Changes
Modify the broadcast receiver in `MainActivity.java`:
- Only trigger `resetMeasurementSession()` (which clears samples and resets timer to 0) when the charger is connected (`Intent.ACTION_POWER_CONNECTED`).
- On charger disconnection (`Intent.ACTION_POWER_DISCONNECTED`), simply call `performSampleUpdate()` to freeze the timer and average calculations at their current state without clearing the session data.
